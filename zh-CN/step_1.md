## 介绍

在这个项目中, 你会学习如何创建一个游戏, 玩家使用控制轮捕捉相应颜色的彩色的小点

\--- no-print \---

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to rotate the controller wheel and catch the flying dots as they reach the centre. If you miss three dots, the game is over.
